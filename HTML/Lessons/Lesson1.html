Lesson 1: Self-Awareness & Emotional Intelligence

What is Self-Awareness & Emotional Intelligence

This video goes over what Self-Awareness and Emotional Intelligenceis and where a person can use them

Ruff Summary

What is Self-Awareness?

Conscious knowledge of one's own character and feelings.

Self-Awareness attributes:
Emotional Self-Awareness
Accurate Self-Assessment
Mindful Self-Reflection

What is Emotional Intelligence

The capacity to be aware of, control, and express one's emotions, and to handle interpersonal relationships judiciously and empathetically.

Emotional Intelligence attributes:
Self-Regulation
Empathy
Social Skills
